## Overview

Collection of data on agricultural land and crop areas, cultivated crops in the territory of the Republic of Lithuania

The download service is a collection of personalized spatial data of agricultural land and crop areas, cultivated crops. 
The service provides object geometry with descriptive (attributive) data.

Geographic information on land use for 2022 is provided, distinguishing between perennial meadows-pastures, 
arable land and perennial plantations.
